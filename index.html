🌍 CURRENT MISSION: SBWF UAE 2026

One of the most exciting chapters of my journey is my participation in the Summer Book Writing Festival (SBWF UAE). SBWF is an inspiring writing experience that celebrates creativity, imagination, storytelling, and the power of young writers. This incredible initiative has expanded across 32 countries, connecting students and aspiring creators through the world of writing. Now, SBWF is being conducted across the entire United Arab Emirates, bringing together a huge community of young participants with around 900 schools and 25,000 participants across the UAE. 🇦🇪✨ Being a part of SBWF is not just about attending an event — it is about becoming part of a larger celebration of creativity, learning, and storytelling. For me, this experience represents another important milestone in my author journey. It is a reminder that every story begins with imagination, and every writer has the power to create something meaningful. 🌟 I'm trying my best for defending my place in top 1 in the best seller league and editing my submitted book's grammar, punctuation and inconsistencies every day, hoping to pass the jury choice evaluation and win that too.. ⚔️
